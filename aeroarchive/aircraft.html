Aircraft Types

Commercial aircraft are used by airlines to carry passengers. Examples include the Boeing 737 and the Airbus A320.

Military aircraft include fighter jets, bombers, and transport planes. Examples include the F-16 and the B-52.

Private planes range from small single-engine aircraft to luxury business jets like the Gulfstream G650.

Historical aircraft include the Wright Flyer, the Spitfire, and the Concorde. These planes changed the course of aviation history.
